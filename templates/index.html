What You Should Know About QR Codes

QR (Quick Response) Codes are two-dimensional barcodes that can store a variety of information like URLs, contact details, or Wi-Fi credentials. They are widely used in business, marketing, and daily life due to their convenience.

How Do QR Codes Work?

QR codes encode data into black and white squares. When scanned using a smartphone or QR code scanner, the data is decoded into actionable information, such as a URL or contact card.

Why Should I Use QR Codes?

QR codes are fast, reliable, and versatile. They are commonly used for sharing links, promotions, event details, or even connecting to Wi-Fi. They are a paperless and eco-friendly solution for sharing information.

How Do I Scan a QR Code?

Most smartphones have a built-in QR code scanner in the camera app. Open your camera, point it at the QR code, and follow the link or action that appears. You can also download QR code scanner apps for older devices.

Tip: Always verify the source of the QR code before scanning, especially if it's from an unfamiliar or suspicious source.
